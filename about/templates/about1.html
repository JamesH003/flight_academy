Focused on Training Excellence

Flight Academy employs approximately 150 permanent staff to deliver training and all associated services. All aspects of the package provided at Jerez are delivered by our own staff, with no elements outsourced. As a result we have total control of the process from accommodation and aircraft maintenance through to ground, flight and simulator training.

At Flight Academy we believe that our instructors are, without exception, highly motivated, charismatic individuals with fascinating aviation backgrounds and a wealth of knowledge and experience. They are passionate about what they do and are committed to help our cadets realise their potential to become exceptional pilots.

Flying School

Flight Academy recruits over 40 fulltime and experienced EASA flight instructors, most of them with 1000’s of hours flying and instructing in international and EASA airspace.
