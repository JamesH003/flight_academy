Focused on Training Excellence

Welcome to Flight Academy. We specialize in Private Pilot License(PPL), Commercial Pilot Licence(CPL) and Airline Transport Pilot License (ATPL) training, providing top-notch education for aspiring aviators. Experience the thrill of flight with our introductory flight vouchers - the perfect gift for aviation enthusiasts. Join us on a journey to the skies, where dreams take flight.

At Flight Academy we believe that our instructors are, without exception, highly motivated, charismatic individuals with fascinating aviation backgrounds and a wealth of knowledge and experience. They are passionate about what they do and are committed to help our students realise their potential to become exceptional pilots.

Our strength is our people

Flying School

Flight Academy recruits over 40 fulltime and experienced EASA flight instructors, most of them with 1000's of hours flying and instructing in Ireland, international and EASA airspace. All our Flight Instructors have successfully passed our thorough standardisation course, deemed to be one of the toughest in the industry, to ensure their training standards are not just in line with our quality system but outstanding. At the heart of our training program is a fleet of meticulously maintained and technologically advanced aircraft. We take pride in offering a diverse range of aircraft types, catering to various training needs. From nimble single-engine planes for initial training to sophisticated multi-engine aircraft for advanced courses, our fleet is a testament to our commitment to providing comprehensive aviation education. Our flight vouchers grant you access to a diverse fleet of aircraft, including the iconic Cessna 152 and Cessna 172, the versatile Piper PA-28, and the sophisticated Diamond DA-42. Each aircraft is meticulously maintained, ensuring your safety and comfort as you soar through the skies.
